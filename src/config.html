⌨ Keymap Configuration

Active keymap: — switch on device with Button 4+1, 4+2, or 4+3 (LED flashes 1/2/3 times to confirm).
Button 4 long-press always enters this config mode (not remappable). Tap Button 4 on the device to exit without saving.
